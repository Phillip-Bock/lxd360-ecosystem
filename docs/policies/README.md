## Policy Lifecycle

1. **Draft** - Initial creation, internal review
2. **Review** - Legal and compliance review
3. **Approved** - Executive approval obtained
4. **Active** - Published and in effect
5. **Under Revision** - Being updated
6. **Retired** - No longer in effect

## Approval Requirements

| Policy Type | Approver |
|-------------|----------|
| Security Policies | CEO + CISO |
| Privacy Policies | CEO + Legal |
| Operational Procedures | Engineering Lead |

## Review Schedule

- **Annual Review**: All policies must be reviewed annually
- **Trigger Events**: Review required after security incidents, regulatory changes, or significant system changes
